Common questions

How is LaunchFast different from Auth0 or Clerk?

Auth0 and Clerk do auth. LaunchFast does auth, billing, onboarding, team management, and analytics in one package. You pick what you need. No need for three separate vendors and four integration weeks.

Can I use just the auth module?

Yes. Every module works independently. Import only what you need. The full stack is there if you want it, but you can run auth alone and pair it with your own billing setup.

What frameworks do you support?

Next.js, Remix, Astro, Nuxt, SvelteKit, and plain Express. The SDK is framework-agnostic at its core, with adapters for the popular ones.

Is there a free tier?

Yes. Free for up to 1,000 active users with full access to auth, billing, and onboarding. No credit card required. Upgrade to Pro when you outgrow it.

How does pricing work for multiple projects?

Each project is billed separately. The free tier applies per project. Pro pricing is per project with a single billing dashboard for your whole account.
